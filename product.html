Experience Cinema-Quality Visuals at Home

The Epson Home Cinema 5050UB brings the magic of cinema directly to your living room with stunning 4K PRO-UHD resolution and advanced pixel-shifting technology. Whether you're watching the latest blockbuster, streaming your favorite series, or gaming with friends, this projector delivers breathtaking image quality that rivals commercial theaters.

With 2,600 lumens of color and white brightness, the 5050UB performs beautifully even in rooms with ambient light. The projector's motorized lens with wide shift range makes installation flexible and hassle-free, while HDR10 support ensures you experience every scene with enhanced contrast and vibrant colors exactly as the director intended.

Epson's 3LCD technology delivers exceptional color accuracy and brightness uniformity across the entire image. Say goodbye to rainbow effects and enjoy smooth, natural color reproduction that makes every viewing session truly immersive. The 5050UB is the perfect centerpiece for your dedicated home theater or multi-purpose entertainment room.
